# Canonical 30–60s Ad Pipeline Documentation

## Overview
This document outlines the streamlined process for creating a 30–60 second advertisement, from initial brief to final delivery. The goal is to ensure clarity, efficiency, and consistency across all ad productions.

## Inputs
- **Creative Brief:** A document detailing the objectives, target audience, key messages, and tone of the ad.
- **Budget:** Financial constraints and resource allocation for the ad production.
- **Timeline:** Key deadlines for each phase of the production process.
- **Brand Guidelines:** Standards for logo usage, color schemes, and overall brand voice.
- **Assets:** Existing media, logos, images, and any other relevant materials.

## Outputs
- **Final Ad:** A polished 30–60 second advertisement ready for distribution.
- **Production Report:** A summary of the production process, including challenges faced and solutions implemented.
- **Feedback Document:** Insights gathered from stakeholders post-delivery for future improvements.

## Steps
1. **Initial Briefing:**
   - Gather stakeholders to discuss the creative brief and objectives.
   - Align on key messages and target audience.

2. **Concept Development:**
   - Brainstorm ideas and create initial concepts.
   - Present concepts for feedback and select a direction.

3. **Scripting:**
   - Draft a script based on the selected concept.
   - Review and revise the script with stakeholders.

4. **Storyboarding:**
   - Create a visual storyboard to outline key scenes.
   - Ensure alignment with brand guidelines and objectives.

5. **Production Planning:**
   - Schedule shoots, secure locations, and hire necessary talent.
   - Prepare a detailed production timeline.

6. **Filming:**
   - Execute the shoot according to the storyboard and script.
   - Ensure quality control during filming.

7. **Post-Production:**
   - Edit the footage, add graphics, and incorporate sound.
   - Review the first cut with stakeholders for feedback.

8. **Final Edits:**
   - Implement feedback and finalize the ad.
   - Ensure all elements align with the creative brief.

9. **Delivery:**
   - Export the final ad in required formats.
   - Distribute to relevant channels and stakeholders.

## Pitfalls
- **Lack of Clarity in Brief:** Ensure the creative brief is comprehensive to avoid misalignment.
- **Insufficient Feedback Loops:** Incorporate regular check-ins to gather feedback throughout the process.
- **Budget Overruns:** Monitor expenses closely to stay within budget.
- **Timeline Delays:** Stick to the production schedule and address issues promptly to avoid delays.
- **Ignoring Brand Guidelines:** Always refer back to brand guidelines to maintain consistency.

By following this pipeline, teams can create effective advertisements that resonate with the target audience while adhering to timelines and budgets.
