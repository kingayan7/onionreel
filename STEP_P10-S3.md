# OnionReel v1 Artifact: P10-S3 - Render 30s Master with Text Cards + End Card

## Overview
This artifact outlines the process for rendering a 30-second master video that includes text cards and an end card. The goal is to produce a polished video suitable for distribution, ensuring clarity and engagement through effective text integration.

## Inputs
- **Video Clips**: Raw footage to be used in the master video.
- **Text Card Content**: Pre-defined text for each card (e.g., titles, subtitles, calls to action).
- **End Card Design**: Visual elements and text for the end card (e.g., logo, social media handles).
- **Audio Track**: Background music or voiceover to accompany the video.
- **Editing Software**: Software capable of video editing (e.g., Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro).

## Outputs
- **Rendered Video File**: A 30-second master video file in the desired format (e.g., MP4, MOV).
- **Text Card Assets**: Individual text card designs used in the video.
- **End Card Asset**: The final design of the end card.

## Steps
1. **Gather Inputs**: Collect all video clips, text card content, end card design, and audio track.
2. **Create Project**: Open editing software and create a new project.
3. **Import Assets**: Import all video clips, audio tracks, and text card designs into the project.
4. **Arrange Clips**: Place video clips on the timeline in the desired order.
5. **Add Text Cards**:
   - Insert text cards at appropriate intervals.
   - Ensure text is legible and aligns with the video's theme.
6. **Insert End Card**: Add the end card design at the conclusion of the video.
7. **Sync Audio**: Adjust audio levels and sync with video content.
8. **Review & Edit**: Watch the video for flow, timing, and clarity; make necessary adjustments.
9. **Render Video**: Export the final video in the required format and resolution.
10. **Quality Check**: Review the rendered video for any errors or issues before distribution.

## Pitfalls
- **Text Legibility**: Ensure text cards are readable against the video background; avoid overly complex fonts or colors.
- **Timing Issues**: Text cards should be displayed long enough for viewers to read but not so long that they disrupt the video's flow.
- **Audio Levels**: Balance audio levels to prevent background music from overpowering voiceovers or important audio cues.
- **Export Settings**: Double-check export settings to ensure the video meets the required specifications for distribution (resolution, format).
- **Feedback Loop**: Seek feedback from team members before finalizing to catch any overlooked issues.

This artifact serves as a concise guide to efficiently render a high-quality 30-second master video with text cards and an end card, ensuring a professional final product.
