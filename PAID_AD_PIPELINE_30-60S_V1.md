# OnionReel Canonical 30–60s Paid Ad Pipeline v1 SOP

## Scope + Assumptions
- This SOP is platform-agnostic and applies to Meta, TikTok, and YouTube.
- Assumes familiarity with video production and editing software.
- Assumes access to necessary brand assets and footage.

## Inputs
- Brand guidelines
- Offer details
- Footage (existing and new)
- Assets (logos, images, music, etc.)

## Outputs
- Master timeline
- Final exports (30s, 60s)
- Cutdowns (15s, 6s)
- Captions (SRT files)

## End-to-End Steps

1. **Brief**
   - Gather brand guidelines and campaign objectives.
   - Confirm target audience and key messaging.

2. **Hook Ideation**
   - Brainstorm 3-5 engaging hooks.
   - Select the strongest hook for the ad.

3. **Script**
   - Write a concise script (30-60s).
   - Include key messaging and call-to-action.

4. **Storyboard**
   - Create a visual storyboard based on the script.
   - Outline key scenes and transitions.

5. **Shot List**
   - Develop a detailed shot list.
   - Include camera angles, locations, and props.

6. **Production Capture**
   - Schedule and conduct the shoot.
   - Capture all necessary footage according to the shot list.

7. **Edit Assembly**
   - Import footage into editing software.
   - Assemble the master timeline based on the storyboard.

8. **Sound/Music**
   - Select background music and sound effects.
   - Ensure audio levels are balanced.

9. **Color**
   - Apply color correction and grading.
   - Ensure visual consistency with brand guidelines.

10. **Motion Graphics**
    - Create and integrate motion graphics as needed.
    - Ensure they align with brand aesthetics.

11. **Captions**
    - Generate captions for accessibility.
    - Ensure accuracy and sync with audio.

12. **QC**
    - Conduct a quality check on the master timeline.
    - Review for audio, visual, and content errors.

13. **Exports**
    - Export final versions (30s, 60s).
    - Create cutdowns (15s, 6s).

14. **Naming/Packaging**
    - Use clear naming conventions for files.
    - Package assets for delivery (include SRT files).

## Acceptance Criteria and QC Checklist for Final Exports
- [ ] Ad meets brand guidelines.
- [ ] Audio is clear and balanced.
- [ ] Visuals are high quality and consistent.
- [ ] Captions are accurate and synced.
- [ ] All required formats are exported.
- [ ] No errors in motion graphics.

## Fast Path vs Cinematic Path
- **Fast Path**
  - Focus on speed; minimal revisions.
  - Use existing footage and templates.
  - Limited motion graphics and effects.

- **Cinematic Path**
  - Focus on high production value.
  - Original footage and custom graphics.
  - Multiple rounds of revisions and polish.

## Common Failure Modes + Fixes
- **Failure: Poor Audio Quality**
  - Fix: Use high-quality microphones; check levels during recording.

- **Failure: Off-Brand Visuals**
  - Fix: Review brand guidelines before production; ensure all assets align.

- **Failure: Inaccurate Captions**
  - Fix: Double-check captions against the script; use automated tools for accuracy.

- **Failure: Missing Exports**
  - Fix: Create a checklist of required formats before exporting.

- **Failure: Tight Deadlines**
  - Fix: Prioritize tasks; consider using the Fast Path for quick turnaround.

By following this SOP, teams can efficiently produce high-quality paid ads that meet brand standards and engage target audiences effectively.
