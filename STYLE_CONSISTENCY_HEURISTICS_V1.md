# OnionReel Style Consistency Heuristics v1

## Purpose
Ensure outputs are on-brand and coherent across all variants, enhancing viewer recognition and engagement.

## Heuristic Categories

### Typography
- **Do**: Use brand-approved fonts consistently.
- **Don't**: Mix fonts that clash or are not on-brand.
- **Example**: Headings in Bold Sans, body in Regular Serif.
- **Settings**: Font sizes (e.g., H1: 36px, Body: 16px).

### Color
- **Do**: Adhere to the brand color palette.
- **Don't**: Use off-brand colors or shades.
- **Example**: Primary color #FF5733, secondary #C70039.
- **Settings**: Hex codes for all brand colors.

### Pacing
- **Do**: Maintain a consistent rhythm in edits.
- **Don't**: Use abrupt cuts or overly long pauses.
- **Example**: 1-second cuts for action, 3-second pauses for emphasis.
- **Settings**: Cut duration guidelines.

### Audio Bed
- **Do**: Use approved background music tracks.
- **Don't**: Incorporate unlicensed or off-brand music.
- **Example**: Track A for upbeat, Track B for serious tones.
- **Settings**: Volume levels (e.g., -10 dB for background).

### Transitions
- **Do**: Use brand-approved transitions (e.g., fade, slide).
- **Don't**: Use flashy or distracting transitions.
- **Example**: Crossfade for scene changes.
- **Settings**: Duration of transitions (e.g., 0.5 seconds).

### Framing
- **Do**: Follow brand-specific framing guidelines (e.g., rule of thirds).
- **Don't**: Use inconsistent angles or cropping.
- **Example**: Subject in the right third of the frame.
- **Settings**: Aspect ratio (e.g., 16:9).

### Caption Style
- **Do**: Use brand font and color for captions.
- **Don't**: Use inconsistent sizes or colors.
- **Example**: White text with a black outline.
- **Settings**: Font size (e.g., 20px), outline thickness (e.g., 2px).

### Logo/End-card
- **Do**: Include logo in designated spots.
- **Don't**: Alter logo size or placement.
- **Example**: Logo in bottom right, end-card with brand colors.
- **Settings**: Logo size (e.g., 100px width).

## Encoding a “Style Pack”
- **Settings**: Document all settings for typography, color, pacing, etc.
- **Examples**: Provide visual examples for each category.
- **Do/Don't**: List clear do's and don'ts for each category.

## Scoring Rubric
- **Pass**: All categories meet guidelines.
- **Warn**: 1-2 categories have minor deviations.
- **Fail**: 3+ categories deviate significantly.
- **Triggers**: Specific deviations from settings, examples, or do's/don'ts.

## Automation vs Human Review
- **Automatable**: Color codes, font usage, audio levels.
- **Human Review**: Pacing, transitions, framing, overall coherence.

## Failure Modes + Fixes
- **Inconsistent Fonts**: Revert to style pack settings.
- **Off-brand Colors**: Replace with approved palette.
- **Poor Pacing**: Adjust cuts and pauses per guidelines.
- **Distracting Transitions**: Replace with approved transitions.

## Acceptance Criteria for “Style Consistency v1 Complete”
- All heuristic categories documented and implemented.
- Style Pack created and accessible.
- Scoring rubric applied to at least 5 outputs.
- Automation tools in place for color and typography checks.
- Human review process established for pacing and transitions.
- Feedback loop for continuous improvement initiated.
