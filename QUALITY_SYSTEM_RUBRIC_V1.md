# OnionReel Quality System Rubric v1

## Audience
Film/Ads/Media Production Team Using AI Tools

---

## Rubric Overview

| Criterion         | Score 0 | Score 1 | Score 2 |
|-------------------|---------|---------|---------|
| **Creative Brief**| No brief submitted | Brief lacks clarity | Clear, detailed brief |
| **Script**        | No script submitted | Script is incomplete | Complete, engaging script |
| **Storyboard**    | No storyboard submitted | Storyboard lacks detail | Detailed, clear storyboard |
| **Shot List**     | No shot list submitted | Shot list is incomplete | Comprehensive shot list |
| **Edit Plan**     | No edit plan submitted | Plan is vague | Detailed, actionable edit plan |
| **Final Export**  | No export submitted | Export is unpolished | High-quality, polished export |

---

## Acceptance Criteria

### Creative Brief
- **Clarity**: Clearly outlines project goals and audience.
- **Detail**: Includes key messages and tone.
- **Format**: Follows standard structure.

**PASS Threshold**: Total score of 3 or more.

### Script
- **Completeness**: Covers all scenes and dialogues.
- **Engagement**: Captivates the target audience.
- **Formatting**: Follows industry-standard script format.

**PASS Threshold**: Total score of 3 or more.

### Storyboard
- **Detail**: Illustrates key scenes and transitions.
- **Clarity**: Easy to understand visual flow.
- **Consistency**: Matches script and creative brief.

**PASS Threshold**: Total score of 3 or more.

### Shot List
- **Completeness**: Lists all necessary shots.
- **Detail**: Includes shot types, angles, and duration.
- **Feasibility**: Realistic and achievable within the schedule.

**PASS Threshold**: Total score of 3 or more.

### Edit Plan
- **Structure**: Clear timeline for editing phases.
- **Detail**: Includes notes on transitions, effects, and music.
- **Collaboration**: Identifies roles and responsibilities.

**PASS Threshold**: Total score of 3 or more.

### Final Export
- **Quality**: Meets technical specifications (resolution, format).
- **Polish**: Free of errors (audio sync, visual glitches).
- **Delivery**: Submitted on time and in the correct format.

**PASS Threshold**: Total score of 3 or more.

---

## Common Failure Modes & Fixes

### Creative Brief
- **Failure**: Vague goals.
  - **Fix**: Use specific language; define success metrics.

### Script
- **Failure**: Incomplete scenes.
  - **Fix**: Review each scene for missing elements; use a checklist.

### Storyboard
- **Failure**: Confusing visuals.
  - **Fix**: Add annotations; simplify complex scenes.

### Shot List
- **Failure**: Missing shots.
  - **Fix**: Cross-reference with storyboard and script.

### Edit Plan
- **Failure**: Lack of detail.
  - **Fix**: Break down tasks into smaller, actionable items.

### Final Export
- **Failure**: Technical issues.
  - **Fix**: Conduct a thorough quality check; use export templates.

---

## Scoring Summary

- **Total Score Calculation**: Sum of scores across all rubrics.
- **PASS Criteria**: Achieve a score of 3 or more in each category.
